/BRAG

What is a /brag page?

Most websites have an /about page that tells you someone's background. Some have a /now page that tells you what they're focused on right now.

A /brag page is where you share what you're proud of — your wins, your projects, your journey. It's a public reminder of what you've accomplished and what you're capable of.

Want to lean more? Listen to the Mostly Technical episode that started the movement!

Why have one?

  • → It's a confidence boost — writing down your wins reminds you of your progress.
  • → It's a conversation starter — visitors to your site learn what you care about.
  • → It's a public commitment — declaring your priorities helps you stay focused.
  • → It creates opportunities — sharing your work opens doors.

How do I get listed?

/BRAG is a community-curated directory. To add your brag page:

  1. Create a /brag page on your personal website.
  2. Fork this repo on GitHub.
  3. Add your info to data/people.json.
  4. Add your photo to public/images/people/ and set the image field in data/people.json to the filename (e.g. your-name.jpg).
  5. Open a pull request.
